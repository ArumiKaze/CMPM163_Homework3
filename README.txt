Part A

Rendered text that is interactable. You can rotate the camera and zoom in.
GUI is present to customize the text even more such as changing the text to
a different word, changing size, color, etc.

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Part B

3D SDF generated objects. There are three present in the scene. The middle one 
is a simple sphere. The one on the right is a complex object composed of two 
different objects. Finally, the last one on the very left is a shape that morphs
into a different object.
